Our Story

Started with one temple route in 2017

South India Holidays Trip began as a two-person operation running a single Madurai–Rameshwaram temple circuit for visiting relatives and friends-of-friends. Word travelled faster than we expected, and by the second year we were mapping routes across Tamil Nadu and into Kerala.

Today we run more than 60 itineraries covering temple circuits, Kerala backwaters, Nilgiri hill stations and coastal towns — but every route still starts the same way: someone on our team has actually walked it.
