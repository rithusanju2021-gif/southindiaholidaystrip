Temple Town

Madurai, Tamil Nadu

Home of the Meenakshi Amman Temple and the beating heart of every South Trails temple circuit.

Alleppey, Kerala

Houseboat cruises through palm-lined canals — the signature Kerala backwaters experience.
Hill Station

Munnar, Kerala

Rolling tea estates and misty peaks — a favourite for honeymoon and family hill escapes.
